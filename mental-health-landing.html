Our mission is to break down barriers to mental health care, reduce stigma, and create a supportive community where every student can thrive emotionally and academically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mental health platform

Is my information confidential and secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and follow HIPAA guidelines to protect your privacy. Your conversations with our chatbot are completely confidential, and your personal information is never shared without your explicit consent.

How does the mental health screening test work?

+

Our screening test uses validated psychological assessments to evaluate your mental health status. It takes about 10-15 minutes to complete and provides personalized insights and recommendations based on your responses. The results help you understand your current wellbeing and suggest appropriate resources.

Can the chatbot replace professional therapy?

+

No, our chatbot is designed to provide support and guidance, but it's not a replacement for professional mental health treatment. If you're experiencing severe depression, anxiety, or thoughts of self-harm, we encourage you to seek help from a licensed mental health professional immediately.

Is the platform free for students?

+

Yes! Basic features including the chatbot, community forums, screening tests, and relaxation resources are completely free for all students. We also offer premium features with additional professional support and advanced tools for those who need more comprehensive care.

How do I connect with other students in the community?

+

Simply create your profile and join discussion forums based on your interests and challenges. You can participate anonymously or with your chosen username. Our buddy system also pairs you with compatible peers for one-on-one support.

What should I do in a mental health crisis?

+

If you're experiencing a mental health crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact emergency services immediately (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or go to your nearest emergency room. Our platform provides ongoing support but is not equipped for crisis intervention.

How often should I use the personal diary feature?

+

We recommend daily entries to track your mood and thoughts effectively. However, you can use it as often as feels comfortable for you. Regular use helps identify patterns and triggers in your mental health journey.

Are the professional videos from real doctors?

+

Yes, all professional content is created by licensed mental health professionals, including psychologists, psychiatrists, and counselors. Each professional's credentials are verified and displayed with their content.

Can I use this platform alongside therapy or medication?

+

Absolutely! Our platform is designed to complement professional treatment, not replace it. Many students find it helpful to use alongside therapy or medication as an additional support tool. Always consult with your healthcare provider about your treatment plan.

How do I get started with the platform?

+

Simply click the "Get Started" button to create your account. You'll complete a brief onboarding process, take an optional screening assessment, and then have access to all features. The entire process takes less than 10 minutes.
